ABOUT THE PROGRAM

What is the PG Mock Test Kit (PG-MTK)?

AchievO's PG Mock Test Kit (PG-MTK) is a structured learning and assessment program designed for undergraduate students planning to pursue higher education. The program combines real exam simulations, detailed performance reports, and merit-based scholarships to improve academic performance and increase success in postgraduate entrance examinations.

PROGRAM FEATURES

What's Included in the PG-MTK Program?

30-Day Mock Test Program

Practice consistently with a complete 30-day schedule designed to strengthen your concepts before the scholarship examination.

Performance Analysis

Receive detailed reports showing your scores, progress, accuracy, and overall improvement after every mock test.

Time Management Practice

Learn how to complete competitive examinations efficiently with timed practice sessions.

Exam Strategy

Improve question selection, accuracy, and confidence using expert exam preparation strategies.

Strength & Weakness Reports

Identify subject-wise strengths and focus on areas that need improvement before the final scholarship examination.

Free PG Mock Kit

Eligible students receive a comprehensive PG Mock Test Kit to prepare effectively for postgraduate entrance exams.

ELIGIBILITY

Who Can Apply for the PG-MTK Scholarship?

The AchievO PG Mock Test Kit (PG-MTK) Scholarship Program is open to undergraduate students who aspire to pursue postgraduate education and prepare for competitive entrance examinations. Students from various degree streams are encouraged to participate and showcase their academic potential.

Eligible Students

  • Intermediate / 12th Pass Students
  • Degree Students (B.Sc, B.Com, BA, BBA, BCA & Other UG Courses)
  • 1st, 2nd & 3rd Year Students
  • Students planning for PG-CET & Higher Education
  • Open to students from Andhra Pradesh & Telangana

Required Documents

  • Intermediate / Degree Marks Memo
  • College ID Card
  • Aadhaar Card (if applicable)
  • Recent Passport Size Photograph
  • Basic Academic Information
SCHOLARSHIP EXAM

PG-MTK Exam Pattern

The scholarship examination is designed to evaluate students' aptitude, reasoning ability, English language skills, and general awareness. The assessment follows a competitive examination pattern to help students prepare for future postgraduate entrance tests.

Total Questions 90 Questions
Maximum Marks 100 Marks
Duration 90 Minutes
Subjects Covered Aptitude, English & General Knowledge
Exam Type Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Aptitude

Evaluate logical reasoning, numerical ability, analytical thinking, and problem-solving skills.

English

Test grammar, vocabulary, reading comprehension, verbal ability, and communication skills.

General Knowledge

Questions covering current affairs, general awareness, science, and basic social knowledge.

TERMS & CONDITIONS

Scholarship Conditions

Scholarships are awarded purely on merit and are subject to the policies of the AchievO Scholarship Program. Students must satisfy all eligibility requirements to continue receiving scholarship benefits.

Scholarship Guidelines

  • Scholarships are awarded based on examination performance.
  • Scholarship applies only to tuition fees.
  • Students must maintain good academic performance.
  • Regular attendance is mandatory.
  • Minimum GPA / CGPA of 8.5 is required (where applicable).
  • Scholarship cannot be combined with other promotional offers.

Important Information

Scholarship awards are released according to the official merit ranking prepared after the scholarship examination. The final decision regarding scholarship allocation rests with AchievO. Submission of incorrect information or failure to meet the eligibility criteria may result in cancellation of scholarship benefits.
