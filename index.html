FAQ

Common questions

1. What is the Merit Scholarship Program?
The Merit Scholarship Program identifies talented students through a scholarship examination and provides fee concessions based on performance.

2. Who can apply?
Students from 10th, Intermediate, and Degree levels who meet eligibility criteria can apply.

3. Is there any application fee?
Yes. Application fees vary based on academic level.

4. How can I apply?
Register online, fill the form, submit details, and confirm registration.

5. What subjects are included?
Subjects are based on the student's academic syllabus.

6. What is the mode of examination?
The scholarship exam is conducted online.

7. What is the duration?
Duration is typically 60–90 minutes.

8. Can students attempt from home?
Yes, if conducted online with a stable internet connection.

9. How are winners selected?
Based on exam performance and merit ranking.

10. Does qualifying guarantee admission?
No. It provides eligibility for fee benefits only.

11. What benefits are provided?
Up to 100% tuition fee scholarship and 30 days free EAMCET preparation access.

12. When are results announced?
Results are published on the official website.

13. What if I forget my login credentials?
Use the Forgot Password option.

14. Whom should I contact for technical issues?
Contact the official helpdesk through the support section or email.
